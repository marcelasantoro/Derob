DEROB: the shortest distance between two points

In a moment of deep boredom, someone decided to write "BORED" backwards and discovered "DEROB". Then they found out that "derob" means "doorknob" in Hindi.

A doorknob to new worlds


Just like a doorknob is the gateway to different rooms and experiences, DEROB is your gateway to escape boredom and discover amazing activities.


Each suggestion is a new door that opens, leading you to experiences that range from simple pastimes to memorable adventures.
